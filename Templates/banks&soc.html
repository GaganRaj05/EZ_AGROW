Banks and Societies Helping Farmers in Karnataka

Cooperative Societies

Primary Agricultural Credit Societies (PACS):

Basic units at the village level providing short-term and medium-term credit to farmers.

Karnataka State Cooperative Apex Bank:

Provides financial support to rural and urban cooperative banks.

View Website

Benefits: Offers credit facilities, supports agricultural development, and promotes cooperative banking in rural areas.

District Cooperative Central Banks (DCCBs):

Operate at the district level, providing credit to farmers through PACS.

Offers agricultural loans such as Kisan Credit Card, crop loans, and equipment financing.

Benefits: Flexible loan options, low-interest rates for farmers, and specialized schemes for agricultural development.
